NOTES These prices are for monthly bundles i.e. 30 days.
Setup costs only considers devices that can be used by a SME, a Startup, or a family using more than 5 devices at a time. Though a smartphone can be used to these are not being considered.
The cheapest options do not necessarily offer the best speeds. In addition, unfortunatley what you are told by an ISP isnt what you will always get. But it would be good to find a way to get average speeds for each of the ISPs and add them to this comparison table.
Another thing to consider is what coverage do the ISPs have - selected neighbourhoods in Lusaka vs whole cities/towns across Zambia. Any information on this will be helpful.
Missing info here also is contention ratio (CR) i.e. how many customers of an ISP share a given data capacity/bandwidth. CR works hand in hand with your bandwith e.g. even though your ISP says you have a speed of 10Mbps if the CR is 20:1 be ready to pull out your hair. The best connection to be on is one with a high bandwidth and a low contention ratio.
